About

Made for the dance floor.

Maiumixmaster is a mixing & mastering service built exclusively around commercial dance music — house, tech house, bass and beyond. Every record is treated for the systems it's made for: the club, the festival stage, and the stream.

Working from a fully analog-hybrid room, the focus is loud, clean, and translatable masters that keep your low end tight and your top end open — ready for release-day across every platform.
